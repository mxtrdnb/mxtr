About

The Artist

MXTR is a Producer / DJ from Chelmsford, UK, delivering high-energy, rave-ready Drum & Bass built for maximum impact on dancefloors and festival stages.

His music has torn through some of the world's biggest Drum & Bass events, including Tomorrowland, Let It Roll, Rampage, and Liquicity Festival, with releases on UKF, Liquicity / Ridmic, Riot Records, and Manifest.

MXTR's debut EP Event Horizon received widespread acclaim across the scene, with the breakout single 'Bounce' reaching #17 on the Beatport Drum & Bass chart and #1 on the Beatport DNB Hype chart.

His UKF release 'Lonely' (with Georgie Riot) surpassed 80,000 streams in its first month, contributing to a rapidly growing fanbase of 60,000+ monthly Spotify listeners.
